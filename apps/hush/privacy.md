# Hush privacy policy

Last updated: 2026-05-28

Hush is built to work locally and privately. The app plays sounds that are bundled inside the app, stores your settings on your device, and does not require an account.

## What Hush collects

Hush does not collect personal data.

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or third-party login. It does not send your saved mixes, timer choices, favorites, playback activity, device identifiers, location, contacts, photos, microphone audio, or any other personal information to us.

## What stays on your device

Hush stores the following data locally on your device:

- your current mix and sound volumes
- saved mixes and mix names
- favorite sounds and favorite mixes
- sleep timer fade-out setting
- snooze duration setting
- whether you have completed onboarding
- a small local widget/watch snapshot, such as the current mix name and play/pause state

This data is used only to make the app work. It is not uploaded to a server.

## Network access

Hush does not need the internet to play sounds. All sounds are bundled with the app. The app is designed to work in Airplane Mode.

## Apple Watch and widgets

The iPhone app may share a small playback snapshot with the Hush widget and Apple Watch companion app through Apple's local App Group and WatchConnectivity systems. This is local device-to-device/app-extension communication. We do not receive it.

## Crash reports and diagnostics

Hush does not include third-party crash reporting in this version. If you contact support, you may choose what information to include in your message.

## Children

Hush does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted with a new date.
