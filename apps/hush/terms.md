Hush is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. If you have a sleep disorder, tinnitus, anxiety, hearing concerns, or any other health issue, talk to a qualified professional.

## Safety

Use Hush at a safe volume, especially with headphones. Do not use the app in a way that blocks important sounds when you need situational awareness, such as while driving, cycling, walking near traffic, or caring for another person.
